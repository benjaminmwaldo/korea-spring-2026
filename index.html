Korea

Spring 2026  ·  Ben Waldo

In early 2026 I was sent to Osan Air Base, South Korea on a military assignment. Between work, I had the chance to explore — fortresses, palaces, war memorials, street markets lit at night, and cities that surprised me at every turn. This is what I saw.

Chapter Seven

War Memorial of Korea

Seoul · Yongsan

As an Air Force officer stationed at a base that owes its existence to the Korean War, this one felt personal. Over 36,000 Americans died here between 1950 and 1953.

The memorial tower  ·  The Brothers Statue — an ROK and North Korean soldier embracing, symbolizing the hope for reunification.

The outdoor equipment display — decades of hardware, with N Seoul Tower visible in the background.

A full-scale replica of the Geobukseon (Turtle Ship), Admiral Yi Sun-sin's ironclad warship from 1592  ·  Korean War-era rifles.
